Support Documentation

Find solutions to common issues and learn how to use our platform effectively

Authentication Issues

Session Expired
Your session has timed out after 10 minutes of inactivity for security reasons.

Solution:

  • Click the "Extend Session" button when prompted
  • If already logged out, simply log in again
  • Keep the page active to avoid timeouts
Invalid Verification Code
The verification code you entered doesn't match or has expired.

Solution:

  • Check your email for the most recent code
  • Request a new code if more than 5 minutes have passed
  • Ensure you're entering the code correctly

Information Submission Issues

Invalid License Plate Format
The license plate number you entered doesn't match the required Kenyan format.

Solution:

  • Use the format: KAA 123A
  • Include spaces exactly as shown
  • Use only valid Kenyan plate combinations
Image Upload Failed
Your image couldn't be uploaded due to size or format issues.

Solution:

  • Ensure image is less than 5MB
  • Use only JPG, JPEG, or PNG formats
  • Try compressing the image if it's too large
Too Many Attempts (Error 429)
You've reached the maximum number of allowed attempts for a specific action.

Rate Limits:

  • Registration: 5 attempts per 15 minutes
  • Login: 5 attempts per 15 minutes
  • Email Verification: 3 attempts per 15 minutes
  • Image Uploads: 6 images maximum for car submission

Solution:

  • Wait for the cooldown period to expire (15 minutes)
  • Verify your information before submitting to avoid multiple attempts
  • If using automation tools, ensure they respect rate limits
Submission Guidelines
Important limits and requirements for submitting driver and car information.

Driver Information Limits:

  • Profile Image: Maximum 5MB, JPG/JPEG/PNG only
  • License Classes: Select all that apply
  • Phone Number: Valid format required
  • Experience: Must be a reasonable number

Car Information Limits:

  • Car Images: Maximum 6 images, 5MB each
  • License Plate: Must follow Kenyan format (KAA 123A)
  • Mileage: Must be a valid number
  • Consumption: Must be in L/100km format

Tips:

  • Compress images before uploading to meet size limits
  • Double-check all information before submission
  • Save your progress if you need to gather more information
Verification Process
Understanding the verification process for submitted information.

Process:

  • Submit information according to guidelines
  • Wait for email verification (expires in 5 minutes)
  • Complete verification within the time limit
  • Session remains active for 10 minutes

If Verification Fails:

  • Request a new verification code
  • Check spam folder for verification emails
  • Contact support if issues persist

Account Management Issues

Account and Information Deletion
Understanding your deletion options and their consequences.

Deletion Options:

  • Driver Information Only: Removes your driver profile while keeping your account
  • Car Information Only: Removes your car details while keeping your account
  • Both Information: Removes all your driver and car information
  • Complete Account: Permanently deletes your account and all associated information

Important Notes:

  • All deletions have a 30-day recovery period
  • Account deletion removes access to all services
  • Recovery requires contacting support with your archive ID
  • After 30 days, deletion becomes permanent
Deletion and Recovery
Understanding the process of deleting and recovering your information.

Important Information:

  • Deleted information is archived for 30 days
  • You can choose to delete driver info, car info, or both
  • Contact support within 30 days for recovery
  • After 30 days, deletion becomes permanent

Technical Issues

Connection Problems
Issues with connecting to our services or submitting information.

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try using a different browser
  • Disable VPN if you're using one
